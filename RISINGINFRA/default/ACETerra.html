Description

Sector 12, Noida Residential 2200SQFT 2.50CR ACE Group Noida

ACE Sector 12 is a newly launched residential project situated in the vibrant heart of Sector 12, Noida Extension. This prime location offers convenient access to essential amenities such as top schools, renowned hospitals, shopping centers, and entertainment options. With excellent connectivity to major highways and areas, this project is an attractive option for investors and potential homeowners.


Key Highlights

Prime Location:

Connectivity: Easy access to FNG Expressway, NH-24, and Greater Noida, ensuring smooth travel for residents.
Educational Institutions: Proximity to top schools like Delhi World Public School and DPS Greater Noida.


Healthcare Facilities:Nearby hospitals include Yatharth Hospital, Fortis Hospital, and Kailash Hospital.
Shopping and Recreation: Close to Gaur City Mall, DLF Mall of India, and Spectrum Metro Mall.
RERA Registration:
